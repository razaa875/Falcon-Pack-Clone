Marking another milestone in the journey towards sustainability, Falcon Pack has obtained certifications of FSC & PEFC Chain of Custody Standards (COC).

Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) and Program for the Endorsement of Forest Certification (PEFC) are non-profit organizations, that set high standards to make sure that forests are responsibly managed, socially beneficial, environmentally conscious and economically viable. The core purpose is to protect forests for future generations.

Products labeled as FSC or PEFC Certified means that they meet the "Gold Standard" ethical production, and the manufacturer (Falcon Pack) meets the requirements of FSC or PEFC."
